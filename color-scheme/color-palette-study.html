Accent system for V2 dark variant
Against the black-red WebGL hero, you need accents that read clearly without competing with the shader. White carries 70% of text. These three colors handle the remaining 30% — used sparingly on lines, tags, subtle marks, and interactive states.

terracotta
golden
latte
Primary accent
Terracotta
#E2725B
Active states, hover lines, selected nav indicators, CTA borders. Echoes the red in the shader without matching it exactly.
Warm highlight
Golden
#C4A000
Sparse — featured tags, skill badges, a single underline on a key phrase. Adds warmth without becoming "gold theme."
Muted secondary
Latte
#C8AD8F
Subdued text (dates, captions, metadata), card borders at ~20% opacity, section divider lines. Warm neutral that replaces cold gray.
